# 📊 Financial Reporting & Analysis Dashboard

> An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze a company's **Profit & Loss Statement** and visualize key financial performance metrics across multiple years.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of a company's financial performance by transforming raw financial data into meaningful business insights. It enables users to evaluate profitability trends, operating performance, and expense analysis through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## ✨ Dashboard Features

✅ Gross Profit Analysis

✅ EBITDA Analysis

✅ Operating Profit Analysis

✅ PBIT & Net Profit Analysis

✅ Year-over-Year Comparison (2018–2020)

✅ Sales Performance Tracking

✅ Gross Profit & Net Profit Margin Analysis

✅ Marketing Expense Analysis

✅ Interactive Filters
- 🌍 Region
- 🌎 Country
- 📅 Quarter
- 🗓️ Month

---

## 📈 Key Metrics Included

| Metric | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| 💰 Gross Profit | Revenue after Cost of Sales |
| 📊 EBITDA | Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization |
| 📉 Operating Profit | Profit generated from business operations |
| 💼 PBIT | Profit Before Interest & Tax |
| 💵 Net Profit | Final profitability after all expenses |

## 📌 Business Insights

📈 Sales increased significantly from **2018 to 2020**.

💰 Gross Profit showed consistent growth, indicating improved revenue generation.

📊 EBITDA remained strong, reflecting healthy operational efficiency.

📉 Operating expenses increased over the years, impacting profitability.

💵 Net Profit remained positive but experienced pressure in 2020 due to higher operating expenses and taxation.
